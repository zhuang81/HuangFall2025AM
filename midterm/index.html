LUCY THE TUXEDO

This is my cat, Lucy, she joined our family in May this year and already has become my sleeping buddy. She loves to curl up next to me, under my arms, suck on her nipples and slowly falls asleep. She also stole my pillow and made it her bed and sometimes she would go under my blanket and nap between my leg.

MORE ABOUT HER

She loved the blue feather teaser I got her when she first arrived, she would jump up and down with it. Until she was having too much playing and the feather broke off.

Then I discovered she might like strings more…. Because she chewed up a finger long blue string and swallowed it when I looked away for 1 second. I was super worried, waiting for the string to safely pass through her stomach. days and days, when I forgot about it, I found a familiar looking blue string in her litter box. Never letting her touch string again…

But she quickly found out that hair ties are fun too! Sometimes she would bring all the hair ties she found under the bed to my pillow.

Every morning, instead of my alarm waking me up, i hear Lucy meowing for treats. Sometimes I wish she can be a good kitty and let me sleep for longer but I just can't ever be mad at her.
