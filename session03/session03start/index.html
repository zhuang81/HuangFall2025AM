Cortinarius violaceus, commonly known as the violet webcap or violet cort, is a fungus in the webcap genus, Cortinarius, for which it serves as the type species. It was first described by Carl Linnaeus in 1753 and has undergone several name changes. (source: wikipedia)

"The color of the powdery print, called a spore print, is useful in both classifying and identifying mushrooms".
